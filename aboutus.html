Our Story

How It All Started

Orderly was founded in 2023 by three passionate computer science students who saw an opportunity to revolutionize the food delivery experience in their community. After experiencing inconsistent service quality from existing food delivery platforms, they decided to create a solution that prioritizes customer satisfaction, restaurant partnerships, and delivery efficiency.

What started as a college project quickly gained traction as local restaurants and customers embraced the platform's user-friendly interface and reliable service. Today, Orderly connects thousands of customers with their favorite local restaurants, delivering delicious meals right to their doorstep.

Our commitment to excellence, transparency, and community support has made us the preferred food delivery platform in the region.

Our Mission & Values

At Orderly, our mission is to make food delivery simple, reliable, and enjoyable. We strive to connect people with great food from their favorite local restaurants, all while supporting the local culinary ecosystem.

Quality

We are committed to delivering quality food that meets our high standards. We partner only with restaurants that share our dedication to excellence.

Efficiency

We value your time and strive to deliver your food promptly without compromising on quality or service.

Community

We are dedicated to supporting local restaurants and building a stronger food community that benefits everyone involved.
